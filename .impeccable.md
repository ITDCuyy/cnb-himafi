## Design Context

### Users

- Primary audience is mixed: prospective students, current HIMAFI members, and external partners.
- Users visit to understand HIMAFI identity, values, programs, and ways to connect.
- The homepage should quickly communicate credibility, warmth, and clear organizational direction.

### Brand Personality

- Warm
- Academic
- Community-focused
- Emotional goal: users feel welcomed, informed, and confident that HIMAFI is active and impactful.

### Aesthetic Direction

- Editorial + modern organizational website with clean composition and intentional hierarchy.
- Keep the existing gold accent as the brand signal.
- Use subtle, professional motion rather than dramatic animation.
- Inspiration references include supabase.com and vercel.com for rhythm, polish, and section clarity, adapted to HIMAFI's warmer tone.

### Design Principles

1. Lead with clear narrative hierarchy from identity to action.
2. Combine warm visual tone with structured, institutional readability.
3. Use color and motion strategically to guide attention, not to decorate.
4. Prefer asymmetrical and varied section rhythm over repetitive card grids.
5. Preserve accessibility and responsive behavior as first-class constraints.
